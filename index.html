Bardic Inspiration Guide

Inspiration Mechanics

Learn about the core mechanics and best timing for Bardic Inspiration.

Basic Mechanics:
  • Inspiration Die: d6 (upgrades to d8 at level 3, d10 at level 5, d12 at level 10)
  • Usage Timing: Before making a check
  • Duration: 10 minutes or until used
  • Gaining Inspiration: Through performance, speech, or inspiring actions
Best Usage Timing:
  • Before important skill checks
  • During critical combat attacks
  • At key social interaction moments
  • During important team coordination
Inspiration Strategies

Explore strategies and techniques for different scenarios.

Combat Strategies:
  • Save inspiration for boss battles
  • Prioritize damage dealers
  • Use at critical moments
  • Coordinate with team tactics
Social Strategies:
  • Use before important negotiations
  • Combine with persuasion skills
  • Use during team coordination
  • Perfect timing for performances
